About us

The Spiritual Tree is an independent Non-Profit Organization founded by Mrs. Malti Agrawal and Mrs. Shreya Jhunjhunwala. We provide several techniques which aim to enhance the Mental, Physical, Emotional, and Psychological health of an individual. All you get to learn from us is the key to happiness and self-empowerment. We believe that changes commence from within and that is where mental health plays a crucial role in an individual's life because what you think, is what you become. Our only mission is to help you connect with yourself and prioritize your mental health to find balance in life.
So, join us and create a beautiful shift in your life!
